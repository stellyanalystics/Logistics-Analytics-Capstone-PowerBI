# Logistics Analytics Capstone Project

## Project Overview

This capstone project analyzes logistics operations, fleet performance, customer revenue, and delivery service metrics using Power BI. The objective was to transform a multi-table logistics dataset into interactive dashboards that provide actionable insights for operational efficiency, cost management, and service performance improvement.

The project combines data modeling, KPI development, and business intelligence techniques to evaluate logistics performance and support data-driven decision-making.

## Objectives

* Analyze logistics operations and revenue performance.
* Evaluate fleet utilization and driver productivity.
* Assess fuel efficiency and operational costs.
* Monitor delivery performance and service reliability.
* Identify customer and destination revenue trends.
* Develop actionable recommendations for operational improvement.

## Dashboard 1: Logistics Operations Analysis

### Key Insights

* Generated $298.6M in revenue from 85.4K trips, demonstrating strong operational scale and customer demand.
* Fuel costs reached $95.6M, making fuel one of the largest operational cost drivers.
* On-time delivery performance stood at 55.7%, highlighting opportunities to improve service reliability.
* Revenue was concentrated among a limited number of customers and destinations, increasing dependency risk.

## Dashboard 2: Drivers and Fleet Performance Analysis

### Key Insights

* Driver productivity, trip volume, and fuel efficiency varied significantly across the fleet.
* Revenue contribution differed among drivers, creating opportunities for performance benchmarking.
* Fleet utilization metrics revealed opportunities to improve asset productivity.
* Fuel efficiency analysis provided insights into potential cost-saving initiatives.

## Recommendations

* Improve on-time delivery performance through route optimization and more effective dispatch planning.
* Reduce fuel-related costs through proactive vehicle maintenance and driver coaching programs.
* Monitor driver and fleet performance regularly to identify high performers and underutilized assets.
* Strengthen relationships with high-value customers while diversifying the customer base to reduce revenue concentration risk.
* Establish performance benchmarks based on top-performing drivers and vehicles.

## Business Impact

This analysis demonstrates how business intelligence and analytics can be used to improve logistics operations, optimize fleet performance, control costs, and enhance customer service delivery.
